Sequences

A sequence is a collection of items with a defined and stable order. Sequences are one of the most common data structures. In this section we’re going to look at the basics of sequences:

  • creating them;

  • querying properties of sequences;

  • transforming key methods on sequences, and the distinction between mutable and immutable sequences
